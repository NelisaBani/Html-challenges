The Article element

The article element specifies independent, self-contained content. An article should make sense on its own


The section tag defines sections in a document such as chapters, headers, footers, or any other sections of the document

The footer tag defines a footer for a document or section. A footer element should contain information about its containing element and it typically contains: authorship information; copyright information; contact information; sitemap; back to top links; related documents.
